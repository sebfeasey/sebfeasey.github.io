About

Hi, I'm Seb.

I'm a DN MADE Graphisme student at the Lycée de la Communication in Metz, with a particular interest in editorial design, typography and visual storytelling.

I love creating work that feels thoughtful, engaging and a little unexpected. Whether it's a publication, poster or visual identity, I'm always interested in how design can communicate ideas and connect with people.

This website is a space where I'll be sharing my projects, experiments and creative journey as I continue to learn and grow as a designer.
